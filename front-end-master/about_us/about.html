As someone who is constantly looking for ways to apply quantitative sciences to real-world situations, Intekhab is heavily interested in leveraging statistics to create social benefit in Bangladesh. He focuses on helping non-profit organizations understand the value of data-analysis, and providing them with statistical insight into their data. Intekhab is also passionate about teaching, and is always eager to advise his peers about how seemingly minor ideas from Probability theory and Stochastic processes can be used to optimize simple daily-life scenarios.
